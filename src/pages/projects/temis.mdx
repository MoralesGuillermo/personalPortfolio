## About Temis

Temis, a legal software, is designed with a startup philosophy to help lawyers manage their cases, clients, files, etc. the smart way. With Temis, people in the legal business can focus on their higher priority tasks. 

Temis helps lawyers organize their workloads and have all their resources at a single place and ready to be accessed.

## Project Impact
Temis is revolutionizing the legal industry by helping lawyers organize, cowork, balance and control their workloads with efficiency and effectiveness.

### Control and Organization
With temis, lawyers now can have full control of their cases, prioritize actions, and organize their work and resources to have greater outcomes with their time

### Centralized resources
Temis helps lawyers have centralized modules for all their cases. All a case's files, notes, and information can be accessed in seconds in a single place.

### Billing
Now, lawyers can easily generate their client's invoices and manage their earnings efficiently.
